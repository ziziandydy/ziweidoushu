üìú Historical Origins of Zi Wei Dou Shu

Zi Wei Dou Shu, also known as "Purple Star Astrology" or "Purple Star Destiny Chart", is one of the most important astrological systems in traditional Chinese divination. Its origins can be traced back to the Tang Dynasty, founded by Zhang Guo Lao (Zhang Guo), and later developed by Chen Xi Yi (Chen Tuan) and others.

Zi Wei Dou Shu centers on the Purple Star (Ziwei), combining the Twelve Palaces, Fourteen Major Stars, Six Auspicious Stars, Six Inauspicious Stars, and the dynamic changes of the Four Transformations (Transformation of Wealth, Power, Fame, and Obstruction), forming a complete system of destiny analysis.

Through the development of the Song, Yuan, Ming, and Qing dynasties, Zi Wei Dou Shu gradually became one of the four major divination systems alongside Ba Zi (Four Pillars), Face Reading, and Feng Shui, beloved by scholars and common people alike.
